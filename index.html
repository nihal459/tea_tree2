About Tea Tree Resort

Introducing TeeTree Resort, Munnar! Nestled in the enchanting village of Pappathichola, Surynelly near Chinnakkanal, this newly unveiled sanctuary offers a serene escape into nature's embrace. Cradled in the verdant arms of the majestic Kolukkumalai Mountain, guests are invited to lose themselves in a tapestry of breathtaking views and tranquil landscapes.

With 12 premium rooms, two sumptuous luxury suites, and 2 deluxe accommodations, TeeTree Resort offers an opulent haven for those seeking a peaceful retreat. Here, you may surrender to the mountain’s gentle caress, where serenity and stillness weave a tranquil spell. Ideal for families, couples, or solitary seekers of solace, this hidden gem promises an enchanting odyssey, where every moment becomes a cherished memory amidst nature's grandeur.
